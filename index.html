Joey Crawford Announces He Will Retire From The NBA At The End Of This Season 3/10/2016

Sad sad day if you’re a fan of NBA refs. Say what you want about Joey Crawford, say he’s a troll, say that he makes the game about himself, that he has insane bias and was a Stern henchman for years and year whenever the league needed a certain result, but don’t say he wasn’t entertaining. I’m going to miss him. One of the greats. And by one of the greats of course I mean Joey Crawford always thought you the NBA fan was paying to see him, Joey Crawford, and not the actual game.

Brando's NBA DraftKings Picks3/10/2016

Welcome back to the daily DraftKings picks where we try to get super rich playing daily fantasy basketball. As usual I’ll do a brief recap of my lineups from the week before then throw out some picks that I’m brainstorming for this weekend, and hopefully get some good feedback/tips/picks from you guys either in the comment section or on Twitter @kmarkobarstool. Turned things around a little bit last night. Blog picks were ok – 120 for Harden, Melo and Chalmers. Would have preferred 140+ but at least we got value from Chalmers before he blew his Achilles out. Faded Chalmers and Melo in my main tourney lineup when it looked like they’d be massively owned, and used the money to spend up on Kemba and Holiday instead. That worked out…the value play of Brewer did not. Only 4 games tonight but looks like there are some great plays out there.
